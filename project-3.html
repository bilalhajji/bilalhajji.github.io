Internship project - NormaDev

During the Innovation project at NormaDev, I led the development of an MVP for a startup specialized in the management and analysis of legal documents intended for corporate lawyers.

Github Repository

Project Overview

JURIDICO emerges as a tailored solution to these challenges, leveraging AI technology to enhance the management and analysis of contracts. It significantly reduces the time spent on contract-related tasks, allowing legal professionals to focus more on strategic aspects of their roles. By automating and simplifying contract processes, JURIDICO not only increases efficiency but also augments the overall productivity of legal departments. Our mission is to streamline legal processes, offering a sophisticated solution for contract generation, management, and contextual analysis, backed by the latest in AI technology.
